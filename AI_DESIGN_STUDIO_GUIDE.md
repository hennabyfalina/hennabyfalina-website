# AI Design Studio - User Guide

Welcome to the **AI Design Studio**, a cutting-edge feature of the Art Henna Studio that blends traditional artistry with modern artificial intelligence. This guide explains how to use the studio's features to create, visualize, and customize henna designs.

## Core Features

### 1. AR Hand Tracking (Augmented Reality)
**What it does:** Uses your device's camera to map henna designs directly onto your hands in real-time.
**How to use:**
- Click the **AR Try-On** icon (Smartphone icon).
- Allow camera access when prompted.
- Place your hand in front of the camera.
- The AI will detect your hand and overlay the selected design, adjusting for movement and depth.

### 2. AI Generator (Neural Design Engine)
**What it does:** Generates unique, never-before-seen henna patterns based on your preferences.
**How to use:**
- Click the **AI Generator** icon (CPU icon).
- Select a style (e.g., "Floral", "Geometric", "Bridal").
- Adjust the "Complexity" slider.
- Click "Generate" to see the AI create a pattern from scratch.

### 3. Style Transfer
**What it does:** Takes an existing image or pattern and converts it into a henna-style outline.
**How to use:**
- Click the **Style Transfer** icon (Brush icon).
- Upload a reference image (e.g., a lace pattern, a flower, or a sketch).
- The AI will process the image and output a stencil-ready henna design.

## Using the Interface

### Launching the Studio
1. Scroll to the **AI Design Studio** section on the homepage.
2. Click the **Launch Studio** button.
3. A full-screen immersive interface will open.

### Controls
- **Mode Switcher:** Toggle between AR, Generator, and Style Transfer modes using the icons at the bottom of the screen.
- **Live Preview:** The central viewport shows the real-time feed or generated canvas.
- **Save & Share:** Once you are happy with a design, use the "Save" button to download it or "Share" to post it directly to social media.

## Technical Requirements
- **Camera:** A working webcam or mobile camera is required for AR features.
- **Browser:** Chrome, Firefox, or Safari (latest versions recommended).
- **Internet:** A stable connection is needed for the AI generation features (as they run on our secure cloud servers).
