Hi, I'm Falina

With over 5 years of experience in the art of henna, I've had the privilege of adorning hundreds of brides and clients with intricate designs that tell their unique stories. What started as a passion for traditional art has blossomed into a full-fledged creative studio serving clients across Chennai and beyond.

My journey began with learning from master artists and has evolved into a contemporary practice that respects tradition while embracing innovation. I believe henna is more than just decoration — it's a celebration of joy, love, and cultural heritage.
