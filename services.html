Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for bridal services, and 1-2 weeks for parties and events. During peak wedding season (Oct-Feb), book 1-2 months ahead.

Do you travel for events?

Yes, we travel throughout the city and surrounding areas. Travel charges may apply for locations outside city limits. Contact us for specific location pricing.

Is your henna safe for pregnant women?

Absolutely! We use 100% natural, chemical-free henna that's safe for everyone, including pregnant women. Our paste contains only natural ingredients: henna powder, essential oils, and sugar.

How long does the henna last?

With proper aftercare, our henna typically lasts 1-3 weeks. The color deepens over 24-48 hours and gradually fades. We provide complete aftercare instructions with every service.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 7+ days before the event receive full refund. 3-6 days: 50% refund. Within 48 hours: non-refundable but can be rescheduled based on availability.
