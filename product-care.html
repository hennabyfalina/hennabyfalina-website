Product Care Guide

Tap any card to reveal detailed instructions

Henna Cone Storage

  • Store henna cones in the freezer
  • Keep tightly sealed to maintain freshness
  • Thaw at room temperature before use
  • Do not refreeze repeatedly
Fresh storage = Darker stain 🤎

Henna Aftercare

  • Leave paste on for 6–8 hours (longer = darker stain)
  • Avoid water for 8–12 hours after removal
  • Gently scrape off dried henna — do not wash immediately
  • Keep hands warm for deeper color
  • Stain darkens fully in 24–48 hours

Oil & Balm Use

  • Apply oil, leave 5 mins, then scrape for easier removal
  • Apply aftercare balm after removing paste
  • Reapply balm before washing hands to protect stain
  • Helps deepen and prolong color

Instant Henna Nail Polish

  • Apply on clean, dry nails
  • Let dry completely
  • Apply second coat for deeper shade
  • Avoid water for 1–2 hours

Black Henna Powder (Nails)

  • Mix powder with few drops of water
  • Apply evenly on nails
  • Leave 30–45 minutes
  • Wash off gently
  • Color darkens over time

Quick Tips

  • Always do a patch test 24 hours before use
  • Store products in cool, dry place
  • Check expiry date before use
  • For external use only
